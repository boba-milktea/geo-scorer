# GEO Scorer

**Score your content’s AI visibility in seconds.**

GEO Scorer is a **fully client-side Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) analyzer** that evaluates how well your content performs in AI-driven environments such as ChatGPT, Claude, and other generative engines.

Paste your content, get an instant score, identify weaknesses, and generate **AI-ready prompts** to improve your writing.

No backend.  
No API keys.  
No sign-ups.  
No data storage.

Everything runs **locally in your browser.**

---

## What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Search is evolving. Instead of only ranking on traditional search engines, content now needs to perform well in **AI-generated answers**.

GEO Scorer helps you optimize your content for:

- AI summarization
- LLM answer generation
- AI citation likelihood
- structured knowledge extraction

It analyzes key signals that large language models tend to prioritize when selecting or synthesizing information.

---

## Features

### Instant GEO Score
Evaluate how AI-friendly your content is with a single paste.

### Detailed Score Breakdown
GEO Scorer evaluates several signals including:

- **Readability** — Flesch readability analysis
- **Structure** — headings, formatting, logical flow
- **Factual Density** — presence of concrete information
- **Authority Signals** — references, expertise cues

### Ranked Issue Detection
Problems are prioritized so you know **exactly what to fix first**.

### AI Prompt Generator
Click **“Get AI Suggestions”** and GEO Scorer will generate a **targeted prompt** you can paste directly into:

- ChatGPT
- Claude

The prompt instructs the AI to **rewrite or improve your content based on the detected weaknesses**.

### 100% Private
Your content never leaves your browser.

- No backend
- No tracking
- No stored data

---

## How It Works

1. Paste your content into the analyzer.
2. GEO Scorer instantly evaluates the text using heuristic analysis.
3. The tool produces:
   - a GEO score
   - a category breakdown
   - a ranked list of issues
4. Click **Get AI Suggestions** to generate a prompt tailored to your content.

Paste that prompt into ChatGPT or Claude to generate improvements.
